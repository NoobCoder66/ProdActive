ProdActive POS - Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". You will be asked to enter your username and answer your security questions to reset your password.

How do I add a new product to the inventory? (Admin)

Navigate to the 'Inventory' tab from the main dashboard, then click on 'Register Product'. Fill in the required product details and click 'Submit'.

How do I process a customer order?

From the 'Transaction' tab, click 'Create Order'. Enter the customer's information, then add products to the order. Once all items are added, proceed to checkout.

How can I view sales reports? (Admin)

Go to the 'Report' section from the admin dashboard. You can then view order details and user logs.
